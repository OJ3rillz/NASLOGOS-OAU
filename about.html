What We Are

The department of management and accounting is one of the four departments created when the then institute of administration was converted into a faculty by a special resolution of the university senate on 5th march 1976. prior to this time, it was a unit within the institute providing postgraduate level instructional programmes designed to professionalize the administrative class of government. the b.sc. accounting programme was developed to meet the middle and high level human resources need in accounting for both the private and public sectors of the economy. to date, the department has produced many graduates at both the undergraduate and postgraduate levels who are serving this nation creditably in both private and public organisations.

The nigerian universities accounting students association(nuasa), obafemi awolowo university chapter, is the student body of the department of management and accounting. Nuasa is an association of accounting students put together to produce wholesome professional accountants who are not only academically sound but also well rounded to succeed in our modern and diversified economy. parading some of the best think tanks in the university, nuasa oau is known for excellence, with notable alumni in the nigerian labour market.

Nuasa is committed to bringing out the best in her members through the organisation of several conferences and summits across the nation.
