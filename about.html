What We Are

The Department of Local Government Studies was created in April, 1984 in the Faculty of Administration, Obafemi Awolowo University, Ile-Ife. The department trains middle and high level manpower personnel for the Local Government sector. It offers short term programmes such this department Diploma, Bachelor of Science, Postgraduate Diploma, M.Sc. and Ph.D. Admission requirements to all postgraduate Degree Programmes in this department follow the normal university regulation.

The department in a way of reaching out to students has under it, a student body called National Association of Local Government Students (NASLOGOS). TYPES OF PROGRAMMES

1. Two years Diploma Programme in Local Government (DLG) for serving officers of Local Government and young secondary school graduates.
2. B.Sc. Local Government Studies.
3. Postgraduate Diploma in Local Government (PGDLG)
4. Master of Science Degree in Local Government (M.Sc.)
5. Doctor of Philosophy Degree in Local Government Studies (Ph.D.)


                                 RATIONALE FOR THE INTRODUCTION OF B.SC. DEGREE

The B.Sc. degree programme in Local Government Studies is being introduced to further provide higher academic opportunities for the holder of Diploma in Local Government Studies from Obafemi Awolowo University and other universities as well as fill gap created by the absence of a degree programme in the discipline.
