The official website of The Nigerian Universities Accounting Students Association, Department of Management and Accounting Obafemi Awolowo University, Ile-Ife, Osun State Nigeria. ABOUT US

The Department of Local Government Studies was created in April, 1984 in the Faculty of Administration, Obafemi Awolowo University, Ile-Ife.

The department trains middle and high level manpower personnel for the Local Government sector. It offers short term programmes such this department Diploma, Bachelor of Science, Postgraduate Diploma, M.Sc. and Ph.D. Admission requirements to all postgraduate Degree Programmes in this department follow the normal university regulation.

The department in a way of reaching out to students has under it, a student body called National Association of Local Government Students (NASLOGOS).
