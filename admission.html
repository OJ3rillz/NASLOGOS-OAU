President's Welcome Speech

The official website of The Nigerian Universities Accounting Students Association, Department of Management and Accounting Obafemi Awolowo University, Ile-Ife, Osun State Nigeria. On behalf of my fellow executives and NUASA OAU at large, I hereby welcome you to our website. It is privilege to welcome you to this great citadel of learning built on learning and culture and also to welcome you to our noble department of Management and Accounting. | It is a department that will develop your intellectual capability by imparting theoretical knowledge that will encourage self-reliance in any field you find yourself, to enable you to understand, exploit and adapt to any environment. It will also help make you a world class accountant who can occupy leadership positions both in public and private sectors of the Nigeria and global economy. Our objective is to champion the academic and socio cultural heritage of our members in other to make them stay relevant in this dynamic profession of accountancy and also grow as seasoned accountants that can contribute immensely to the growth and development of our community and the country at large.
