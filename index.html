WELCOME ADDRESS

Akinkunmi Sodiq Opeyemi
President

“The future belongs to those who believe in the beauty of their dreams” – Anonymous

On behalf of the Executives of the National Association of Local Government Students and with the above quote, I consider it a privilege and honor to welcome you to this great citadel known for learning and culture.

You should be proud of yourself having being offered admission to study in the department of Local Government Studies, Faculty of Administration, Obafemi Awolowo University. The place where dreams are built and destinies are fulfilled . The desired place for all students!

However, your admission comes with different expectations from all stakeholders involved i.e. Parents, siblings, lecturers and excos. But then, all of us have just one objective which is to see you succeed.

Take note that there exists a student departmental body called the NATIONAL ASSOCIATION OF LOCAL GOVERNMENT STUDENTS (NASLOGOS). The said Association serve as a link between the management and the students. I urge you to channel all your views to the management through this indivisible and indissoluble body that is guided by laid down constitutional provisions. Of note, all of you are members of the Association.

It is important to note that inappropriate and antisocial behaviors sent the reputation is the department. We want you to be an ambassador of the department anywhere you are both in dressing and behavior. One of they challenges students face generally is the Time Management Issue. Poor time management could cause you to be frustrated and stressed unnecessarily. I wish you all the very best as your spend the next few years in this den of progressiveness.

In the same vein, I welcome all Returning students back to campus and I wish you a stress and hitch free session.

Once again, you all are welcome!

AKINKUNMI SODIQ OPEYEMI

PRESIDENT, DEPARTMENT OF LOCAL GOVERNMENT STUDIES

ABOUT US


The Department of Local Government Studies was created in April, 1984 in the Faculty of Administration, Obafemi Awolowo University, Ile-Ife.

The department trains middle and high level manpower personnel for the Local Government sector. It offers short term programmes such this department Diploma, Bachelor of Science, Postgraduate Diploma, M.Sc. and Ph.D. Admission requirements to all postgraduate Degree Programmes in this department follow the normal university regulation.

The department in a way of reaching out to students has under it, a student body called National Association of Local Government Students (NASLOGOS).
